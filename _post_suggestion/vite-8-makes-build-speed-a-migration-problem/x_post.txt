Vite 8 Rolldown is not interesting because a Rust bundler sounds fast.

It is interesting because frontend teams have spent years paying a tiny attention tax on every rebuild, sourcemap glitch, plugin mismatch, and CI bundle step that takes just long enough to break flow.

Speed is the headline. Trust is the product.

If Rolldown gives Vite one consistent build path without turning migration into a new personality test, that is a real win.

Less waiting. Fewer "works in dev" mysteries. More shipping with your remaining sanity intact.
