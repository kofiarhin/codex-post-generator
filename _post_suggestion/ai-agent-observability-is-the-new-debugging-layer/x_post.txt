AI agent observability is the part of the AI stack that sounds boring until an agent "successfully" finishes a task and leaves you debugging five tool calls, two retries, a slow model response, and one mysteriously confident bad decision.

That is the real production problem.

Not whether agents can demo well.

Whether you can trace what they did, why they did it, what it cost, where latency came from, and which downstream system got dragged into the mess.

Hype ships demos. Observability ships trust.
