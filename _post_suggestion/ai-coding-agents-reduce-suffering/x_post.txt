AI coding agents are useful when they reduce suffering, not when they pretend to replace judgment.

Most of the pain in software work is not typing code. It is tracing weird edge cases, reloading context after interruptions, cleaning up half-finished refactors, and spending an hour proving the bug is nowhere near the line the stack trace blamed.

That is why the good AI developer tools story is still pretty boring in the best way: less repetition, shorter debugging loops, faster reviews, better momentum.

Not “autonomous engineer” theater.

If a tool gives me back focus without asking me to surrender responsibility, that is already a win. #AICodingAgents #DeveloperTools #SoftwareEngineering
