AI model pricing is becoming architecture.

GPT-5.5 is priced above GPT-5.4 at the API level, but OpenAI is arguing token efficiency: fewer retries, fewer wasted tokens, better long-running work.

Anthropic's Opus story is the mirror image: the rate card can stay familiar while tokenizer behavior changes the actual bill.

The long-term effect is boring and important.

Teams will need model routing, prompt caching, evals, quota alerts, and cost dashboards the same way they need logs.

"Which model is best?" is turning into "which model earns its spend on this workflow?"
