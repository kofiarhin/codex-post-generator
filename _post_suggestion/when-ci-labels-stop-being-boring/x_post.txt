GitHub Actions image migrations are where CI reminds you it has a life of its own.

Your app code does not change. Your lockfile does not change. Then `windows-latest` picks up a new Visual Studio default, or `macos-latest` drifts into a new SDK, and suddenly the build fails on something deeply unromantic.

A hosted runner label is not just a convenience. It is an operational contract with an update schedule.

Pin what must be stable. Test what will move.
